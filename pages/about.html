Learn About AllStyle Homes

AllStyle Homes is a family owned business operated by Alex and Sheree Knox.

Alex has spent over 25 years in the building industry and together, with Sheree has operated AllStyle Homes for the past 15 years. Alex has a hands-on approach to the business spending much of his time on the job sites.

Sheree is a qualified interior designer with a flair for creating designs that continue to wow their clients. Sheree works closely with each client to ensure that the design fits perfectly with the client’s needs. In addition Sheree also looks after the business/finance side of the operation as well as taking a direct role in the business marketing efforts.

Alex and Sheree have worked hard to build a business on old-fashioned values and service to ensure they deliver high quality homes every time.

From the moment you step into our office to the day you move into your new home, AllStyle Homes is there every step of the way. We don't just build homes. We build relationships. Let us turn your dream into a reality.



Mission, Vision and Values

Our Mission, Vision and Values help guide the way we behave toward our customers and each other, every day. These are more than mere statements – they form the defining elements of AllStyle Homes' culture.

Mission Statement

To deliver the highest-quality workmanship possible while still remaining cost-effective and on schedule by employing and supporting motivated, flexible, and focused teams. We value the importance of our relationships and will continue to remain fair and true in our dealings with all employees, clients, vendors, and partners. Our clients count on our dependability, our drive, and our integrity. We take great pride in our accomplishments and build on them every day.

Vision Statement

We are a dedicated team striving to bring growth to our community, helping to support existing companies and assist our clients in making their dreams become a reality. Through teamwork, responsiveness and a vast wealth of industry knowledge, we will make AllStyle Homes your partner in building the future together.

Values Statement

AllStyle Homes is a company that is committed to the traditional and professional values. These are vital elements in earning the respect and trust of our valued clients and associates. Strong values also help to ensure a cophesive, collaborative, focus-driven staff.

  • Excellence – We aim for commitment and excellence in the design and construction of major projects. Maximising client satisfaction while respecting the environment is the essence of all our actions, from the most simple to the most highly technical.
  • Integrity – We will conduct business in a manner that demonstrates high standards of moral and ethical behaviour as individuals and as a company. We consistently do what is ethically right. Our word is our bond. We can be counted on to do what we say and follow through with our commitment. Honesty – Our ethical behaviour is above reproach.
  • Employee recognition – Our greatest asset is the men and women we employ. They work as a team, towards the same goal. Our company trains, listens to and communicates with its employees, and makes a point of recognising the contribution of each of them.
  • Teamwork – Teamwork is the way we get things done at AllStyle Homes, and at the heart of working together is respect and support for one another, regardless of role or team.
  • Individual responsibility – The men and women working on our projects enjoy a wide measure of autonomy and have all the means they need to do their job. In return, we expect loyalty and transparency.
  • Safety – We will provide a safe work environment for our employees and clients through proactive training, focus, and recognition. The health and welfare of our employees is critical to our success.
  • Accountability – We will be there for our clients. We will be responsive to all stakeholders and stand behind our work. We will fulfil our commitments to our clients, vendors, and employees.
  • Community – We will have a positive impact in all of our communities. Our community involvement is all about having an impact, not merely playing a role. And it refers to all communities – whether they are neighbourhoods, cities or professional bodies.
  • Innovation – Since every project is unique, we harness our creativity to find customised solutions that meet even the most complex requirements of our clients and the end-users.
